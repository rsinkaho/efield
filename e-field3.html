How to Interact

  1. Move Charge: Drag the test charge on the canvas to see the field and force vectors change in real-time.
  2. Plan Trajectory:
    • Desktop: Click and drag from the test charge.
    • Touchscreen: Touch and hold (long press) for a moment, then drag.
  3. Release to cancel the projection.
